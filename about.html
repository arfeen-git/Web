About Us

Our Story: Preserving History, One Coin at a Time

Welcome to Antique World, where passion meets history! Our journey began years ago, fueled by a deep fascination for the stories hidden within old coins and banknotes. What started as a personal hobby quickly blossomed into a mission to preserve and share the rich tapestry of human history encapsulated in these precious artifacts.

Our founder, Anila Asif, recalls the moment when the spark was ignited. It was a chance encounter with a rare coin tucked away in a forgotten corner of an antique shop. As the coin exchanged hands, so too did a piece of history, carrying with it tales of distant lands, bygone eras, and the hands it passed through over the centuries.

Driven by a desire to unravel these mysteries and connect with the past, Anila Asif embarked on a journey of discovery. Hours were spent poring over dusty tomes, scouring flea markets, and engaging with fellow enthusiasts, each encounter deepening the appreciation for the intricate artistry and historical significance of numismatics.

Whether you're a seasoned collector seeking a rare addition to your treasury or a newcomer eager to explore the world of numismatics, we are dedicated to providing a seamless and enriching experience.

But Antique World is more than just a marketplace; it's a community united by a shared love for history and a reverence for the artifacts that embody it.
